Key Achievements

Tree Canopy Delineation & Species Prediction

This example showcases the progress we have made during the seed phase of the project. Using high-resolution satellite imagery, we developed an automated AI Deep Learning model capable of segmenting individual tree crowns, delineating canopy areas, and counting the number of trees with remarkable accuracy.

In the top-left panel, the INTERVAL tree crown segmentation results were compared with the BlueSky commercial data product. Our developed model demonstrates superior accuracy and detailing, highlighting the significant improvement over existing commercial solutions.

The bottom-left panel compares our tree crown assessment with Google's tree canopy product, showcasing the reliability and accuracy of the data extracted by our model. For this task, we collaborated with Google, gaining access to their canopy data, which is currently unavailable for many European cities. Despite this, our model outperformed Google's product in identifying individual tree crowns and canopy areas.

The right panel presents the outcomes of our tree species classification assessment using our Deep Learning model. The model successfully classified trees into different species categories with a high level of accuracy. These outputs were verified against field-collected data to ensure reliability, usability, and the overall quality of the information produced.

Green View Index Evaluation

This is another example of the work we have done during the seed phase using street view images collected from a 360 degree camera.

For this work, we have collaborated with Meta/Facebook, and they have provided us with the right equipment to collect the street level information, using this tool, we have collected streetlevel images for almost all the streets of Crumlin, which accounts more than 80km and more than 30,000 images were recorded.

Using the above information, we have quantified the green view index for all the public roads of Crumlin. The higher values are indicating the higher amount of green space, street trees, tree canopy along the streets, and vice versa. This type information is extremely useful for understanding the quality of street landscape, urban green infrastructure.

Apart from that, we also have developed the design the prototype of our most ambitious and anticipated product - i.e. INTERVAL Digital Tree Information System.
